Our History

From a Single Bean to a Blooming Business

Our journey began not in a bustling cafe, but with a single, potent idea nurtured by a deep love for coffee. It started in 2024, when our founder, Ibrahim Khan, a passionate connoisseur with a dream, envisioned a space where the rich aroma of freshly roasted beans could mingle with the warmth of community.

What began as humble beginnings, perhaps a small roaster in a garage or a pop-up stall at a local market, was fueled by an unwavering dedication to sourcing the finest beans from around the globe and perfecting the art of the roast. Each batch was a labor of love, a testament to the belief that coffee is more than just a beverage – it's an experience, a connection, a daily ritual that brings joy and inspiration.
